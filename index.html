📖 History of Indian Cricket

  • India played its first Test match in 1932 at Lord's against England.
  • The captain of the team was C. K. Nayudu.
  • Cricket later became the most popular sport in India.
  • India's first major international victory came in 1983.

🏆 Team Achievements

Total ICC Trophies Won: 6

  • ODI World Cup – 2 titles
    • 1983 – Captain Kapil Dev
    • 2011 – Captain MS Dhoni
  • T20 World Cup – 2 titles
    • 2007 – Captain MS Dhoni
    • 2024 – Captain Rohit Sharma
  • ICC Champions Trophy – 2 titles
    • 2013 – Captain MS Dhoni
    • 2025 – Captain Rohit Sharma

India is one of the most successful cricket teams in the world and has won all major ICC tournaments.

⭐ Famous Indian Players

Sachin Tendulkar

  • Known as the "God of Cricket".
  • Scored 100 international centuries.
  • Played international cricket for 24 years.

MS Dhoni

  • Nicknamed "Captain Cool".
  • Only captain to win all three ICC trophies.
  • Famous for finishing matches under pressure.

Virat Kohli

  • One of the best batsmen of the modern era.
  • Former captain of the Indian team.
  • Known for consistency and aggressive play.

Smriti Mandhana

  • Star player of the Indian Women's Cricket Team.
  • Left-handed opening batter.
  • ICC Women's Cricketer of the Year winner.

📌 Why Cricket is Popular in India

  • Massive fan following across the country.
  • Historic victories in ICC tournaments.
  • Professional leagues like the IPL.
  • Legendary players who inspire young cricketers.
